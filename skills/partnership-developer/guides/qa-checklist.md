# QA Checklist — Partnership Developer

Use this before finalizing any partnership-focused output.

## 1. Objective clarity
- Is the business objective explicit?
- Does the answer explain why partnership is the right lever?
- Is the partnership type matched to the goal?

## 2. Mutual value quality
- Is the partner's incentive clear and credible?
- Does the recommendation avoid one-sided “they should help us” logic?
- Is there a reason the partner would engage now?

## 3. Prioritization realism
- Are targets ranked with explicit criteria?
- Are access difficulty, implementation burden, and internal readiness considered?
- Did the answer avoid vanity targets with no activation path?

## 4. Pilot and execution design
- Is there a clear first win, pilot, or first ask?
- Are enablement, technical, support, and attribution needs acknowledged?
- Are ownership and next steps clear?

## 5. Risk awareness
- Are channel conflict, exclusivity, dependency, and support burdens considered where relevant?
- Are assumptions and unknowns called out?
- Does the result avoid overbuilding the partnership before proof exists?

## 6. Output usefulness
- Is the result actionable for outreach and internal alignment?
- Are the value proposition and ask concise?
- Would a team know who to target, what to propose, and how to measure success?
