# QA Checklist — Mergers Analyst

- Strategic rationale is explicit and compared with build or partner alternatives.
- Output distinguishes verified diligence from management claims.
- Revenue quality, concentration, and QoE concerns are treated seriously.
- Synergies are separated into cost, revenue, and negative effects.
- Valuation uses ranges and scenario logic rather than single-point precision.
- Risks are translated into price, structure, or integration implications.
- Final recommendation is crisp: proceed, renegotiate, gate, or walk.
