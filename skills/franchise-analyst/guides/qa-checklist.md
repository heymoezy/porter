# QA Checklist — Franchise Analyst

Use this before finalizing any franchise-analyst output.

## 1. Scope and franchise fit
- Is the request actually franchise-specific rather than generic small-business or real-estate work?
- Is the operator type and decision context explicit?
- Are evidence gaps, especially around FDD data or franchisee interviews, stated clearly?

## 2. Economics quality
- Are investment needs, fee stack, and working-capital burden covered?
- Are mature-unit and ramp-stage economics distinguished?
- Are owner earnings, payback, and downside survivability addressed realistically?

## 3. System and incentive analysis
- Are franchisor and franchisee incentives examined separately?
- Are closures, transfers, support quality, and saturation risk considered?
- Is management bandwidth addressed for multi-unit or semi-absentee cases?

## 4. Evidence and boundaries
- Are claims grounded in provided materials or labeled as judgment?
- Is Item 19 or other FDD evidence handled carefully without legal overreach?
- Are legal, accounting, or site-selection specialist needs flagged where relevant?

## 5. Deliverable usefulness
- Would an operator or investor know the next diligence steps?
- Are deal breakers and conditional assumptions obvious?
- Is the structure easy to scan and decision-ready?
