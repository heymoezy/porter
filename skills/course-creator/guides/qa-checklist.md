# QA Checklist — Course Creator

Use this before finalizing any course design.

## 1. Outcomes and alignment
- Are learner outcomes explicit, observable, and appropriate for the audience?
- Does each module contribute to those outcomes?
- Do assessments actually test the intended skills or knowledge?

## 2. Sequencing and pacing
- Is the material ordered from foundational to more demanding work?
- Is the pacing realistic for the format and time budget?
- Did you avoid overloading single modules or sessions?

## 3. Learning design quality
- Is there meaningful practice, not just explanation?
- Are feedback loops, self-checks, or evaluation points included?
- Does the design support transfer to real use, not just memorization?

## 4. Delivery fit
- Does the structure match live, async, cohort, or blended delivery realities?
- Are facilitation notes, materials, or learner instructions sufficient?
- Are accessibility or prerequisite considerations visible where relevant?

## 5. Boundary discipline
- Is this instructional design rather than simple docs, slide polish, or LMS admin?
- Would an instructor or builder be able to execute from this output?
- Did you avoid returning a topic list disguised as a course?
