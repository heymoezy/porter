# QA Checklist — EdTech Designer

Run this before finalizing any learning-design output.

## 1. Outcome quality
- Are learning goals observable and testable?
- Is the expected mastery level clear?
- Would a builder know what learners must do by the end?

## 2. Sequencing quality
- Does the design move coherently from explanation to practice to feedback?
- Is complexity scaffolded instead of dumped?
- Is cognitive load managed intentionally?

## 3. Practice quality
- Do learners retrieve, apply, decide, or produce something meaningful?
- Are examples, supports, and feedback strong enough for the intended learner?
- Is there enough repetition or spaced return for important knowledge?

## 4. Assessment alignment
- Does assessment measure the stated outcome rather than an easier proxy?
- Are formative and summative checks used appropriately?
- Would the evidence generated reveal real learning transfer?

## 5. Context fit
- Are time, platform, accessibility, and support constraints addressed?
- Is motivation and persistence designed into the experience?
- Is the recommendation realistic for the environment?

## 6. Deliverable strength
- Is the output concrete enough to implement?
- Are assumptions and risks visible?
- Does the design optimize for transfer rather than completion theater?
