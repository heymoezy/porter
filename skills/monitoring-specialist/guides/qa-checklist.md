# QA Checklist — Monitoring Specialist

- Monitoring is anchored to user journeys, service boundaries, and likely failure modes.
- Paging alerts are actionable, owned, deduplicated, and tied to clear user impact or urgent risk.
- Metrics, logs, traces, and deploy context can be correlated during triage.
- Dashboard sections answer health, blast radius, likely cause, and next-step questions.
- Cardinality, retention, sampling, privacy, and telemetry cost are addressed explicitly.
- Synthetic or black-box checks are included where internal signals are insufficient.
- Output includes validation steps for both true positives and false-positive resistance.
- Recommendations are concise, concrete, and free of tool-worship or filler.
