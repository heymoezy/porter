# QA Checklist — GoG

## 1. Object accuracy
- Did you identify the correct Workspace app and object type?
- Did you verify the exact file, thread, event, sheet, or contact before acting?

## 2. Scope control
- Was the query or update as narrow as possible?
- Did you avoid broad or ambiguous writes when a scoped action would work?

## 3. Traceability
- Did you preserve useful IDs, links, titles, dates, recipients, tabs, or ranges?
- Could another operator audit what happened quickly?

## 4. Write safety
- If a write happened, is the changed object clearly named?
- Did you call out partial success, permission issues, or ambiguity?

## 5. Handoff usefulness
- Is the result packaged as something usable: shortlist, summary, created object, or update report?
- Would the user avoid repeating discovery work?

## 6. Scope discipline
- Did you stay in Google Workspace operation rather than drifting into general research or deep analysis?

## 7. Finish quality
- Is the output concise, concrete, and operational?
- Are next steps clear when the action could not be completed fully?
