# QA Checklist — Storyboard Artist

Use this before finalizing.

## 1. Brief fit
- Is the sequence objective explicit?
- Are runtime, format, and production constraints clear enough?
- Are missing details handled through stated assumptions rather than invention?

## 2. Sequence clarity
- Can a cold reader follow who is where, what changes, and why it matters?
- Are screen direction, eyelines, entrances, exits, and reveals coherent?
- Does each panel earn its place?

## 3. Coverage quality
- Do shot choices serve orientation, emotion, or information rather than decoration?
- Is the board economical instead of overcovered?
- Are pacing and transition choices visible on the page?

## 4. Production usefulness
- Could a director, animator, producer, or editor act on this immediately?
- Are continuity hazards or impractical asks called out?
- Does the board fit likely shoot / animation realities?

## 5. Honesty and judgment
- Are assumptions, risks, and unknowns explicit?
- Did the work avoid cinematic cliché and fake precision?
- Does it meet the quality bar: understandable, purposeful, coherent, economical, handoff-ready?
