# QA Checklist — vendor-manager

Use this before finalizing any vendor-focused output.

## 1. Decision clarity
- Is the buying, renewal, replacement, or governance decision explicit?
- Are must-haves, constraints, and disqualifiers visible?
- Is the recommendation tied to a real business outcome?

## 2. Comparison quality
- Are vendors compared on the same criteria?
- Were criteria weighted or clearly prioritized?
- Did the analysis include implementation effort, TCO, and operating burden?

## 3. Risk discipline
- Are lock-in, switching cost, and data portability covered?
- Are SLA, support, and service-model risks visible?
- Are renewal deadlines, notice periods, or auto-renewal traps surfaced?

## 4. Recommendation usefulness
- Does the output clearly say choose, shortlist, negotiate, remediate, renew, or exit?
- Are tradeoffs explicit rather than implied?
- Are negotiation points or next actions concrete?

## 5. Output quality
- Is the answer concise and meeting-ready?
- Would an operator know what to do next?
- Is uncertainty named where evidence is weak?
