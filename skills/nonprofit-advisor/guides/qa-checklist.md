# QA Checklist — Nonprofit Advisor

Use this before finalizing any nonprofit-focused output.

## 1. Mission grounding
- Is the mission and beneficiary group explicit?
- Does the advice protect mission effectiveness rather than just growth?
- Are impact claims supportable?

## 2. Role clarity
- Are governance, management, fundraising, and program decisions clearly separated?
- If the board is involved, are board-only responsibilities defined?
- Is accountability assigned clearly enough to act?

## 3. Sustainability realism
- Does the answer reflect budget, staffing, and execution capacity?
- Are revenue concentration and unrestricted/restricted funding issues considered?
- Are founder dependence or succession risks surfaced if relevant?

## 4. Fundraising quality
- Are revenue channels matched to readiness and donor fit?
- Are tradeoffs among grants, donors, recurring giving, partnerships, and earned revenue explicit?
- Does the recommendation avoid undisciplined channel sprawl?

## 5. Evidence and risk
- Are data gaps and assumptions named?
- Are legal, tax, or jurisdiction-specific issues clearly flagged as requiring qualified counsel when relevant?
- Are partnership, reputational, or operational risks surfaced where they matter?

## 6. Output usefulness
- Is the output concrete and prioritized?
- Would a nonprofit team know what to decide or change next?
- Is the advice concise, practical, and free of sector cliches?
