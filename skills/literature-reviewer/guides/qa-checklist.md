# QA Checklist — Literature Reviewer

- The deliverable is a true literature synthesis, not a stack of disconnected paper summaries.
- The review question, scope boundaries, and review type are explicit.
- Inclusion / exclusion logic or corpus boundaries are transparent.
- Cross-study comparison covers methods, samples, contexts, outcomes, and limitations where relevant.
- Evidence strength is assessed honestly; robust findings are separated from tentative signals.
- Contradictions are explored rather than averaged away.
- Review limitations are named: partial search, limited access, publication bias, heterogeneity, or weak study design.
- Gaps and next research questions follow from the corpus rather than generic “more research is needed” filler.
