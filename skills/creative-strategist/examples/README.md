# Example Requests — Creative Strategist

Use this skill for asks like:

- “Turn this rough launch brief into three campaign territories with a recommendation.”
- “We sell procurement software to CFOs. Find a positioning angle that does not sound like generic efficiency software.”
- “Build a messaging platform and content pillars for a premium travel brand entering a crowded category.”
- “Reframe our webinar series so it feels insight-led instead of standard B2B demand gen.”
- “Give me distinct concept directions for a new skincare line and explain which one scales best.”

## Example output shapes

### 1. Creative brief
- business objective
- audience
- insight or tension
- proposition
- proof points
- tone and guardrails
- success criteria

### 2. Territory matrix
- territory name
- premise
- why it could work
- sample hooks
- best-fit channels
- tradeoffs or risks

### 3. Messaging platform
- master narrative
- value pillars
- reasons to believe
- emotional payoff
- headline or tagline directions
- channel implications
