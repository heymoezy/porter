# QA Checklist — Feedback Analyst

Use this before finalizing.

## 1. Fit
- Is this fundamentally feedback synthesis rather than survey design, market research, ETL, or quant-only analysis?
- Is the business decision or audience explicit?
- Are adjacent-skill boundaries respected?

## 2. Dataset discipline
- Are sources, time range, sample size, and segmentation assumptions stated?
- Are sample limitations and channel bias explicit?
- Is the analysis careful about representativeness?

## 3. Theme quality
- Are themes actionable and clearly separated?
- Are bugs, requests, confusion, trust concerns, and messaging issues distinguished where needed?
- Did the analysis avoid mixing symptoms with root causes?
- Are frequency and severity both considered?

## 4. Evidence quality
- Are major claims backed by examples or quotes?
- Are confidence levels or uncertainty notes included where appropriate?
- Are weakly supported hypotheses labeled as hypotheses?
- Did the output avoid overclaiming from noisy samples?

## 5. Decision usefulness
- Does the output clearly rank or frame what matters most?
- Are recommendations tied to evidence strength?
- Would product, support, or leadership know what to do next?
- Is the result concise without sacrificing credibility?
