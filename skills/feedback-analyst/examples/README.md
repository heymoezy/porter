# Feedback Analyst — Example Output Shapes

Use these as patterns for strong deliverables.

## Example 1 — Support corpus synthesis

**Input:**
Analyze 800 support tickets from the last 30 days to identify the most important product pain points.

**Strong output shape:**
- dataset limits and segmentation notes
- theme table with frequency, severity, evidence, and confidence
- distinction between bugs, usability friction, and policy confusion
- prioritized actions for product and support teams

## Example 2 — Launch reaction review

**Input:**
Synthesize survey comments, app-store reviews, and community posts after a major feature launch.

**Strong output shape:**
- channel-bias caveats up front
- split between excitement, confusion, trust concerns, and onboarding gaps
- representative quotes by segment
- recommendation set covering product fixes versus messaging fixes

## Example 3 — Churn interview memo

**Input:**
Review 25 churn interviews and create a memo for leadership on why customers leave in the first 90 days.

**Strong output shape:**
- segment-aware root-cause synthesis
- severity-weighted interpretation rather than quote counting
- hypotheses for onboarding, pricing, and reliability drivers
- top three interventions plus open questions for further research
