# QA Checklist — Copywriter

Use this before finalizing any copy.

## 1. Audience and action
- Is the audience specific enough to shape the wording?
- Is the desired action obvious?
- Does the copy match the awareness stage and channel?

## 2. Message strength
- Is the core promise clear and credible?
- Is differentiation visible, not implied vaguely?
- Are the strongest benefits concrete rather than generic?

## 3. Objections and proof
- Did you address likely hesitation, risk, or inertia?
- Are proof points real, supportable, and well-placed?
- Did you avoid unsupported superlatives or compliance-risk claims?

## 4. Copy craft
- Does the opening earn attention fast?
- Is the hierarchy easy to scan?
- Did you cut filler, repetition, and weak adjectives?
- Is the CTA direct and specific?

## 5. Boundary discipline
- Is this still copywriting, not broad strategy theater or general content drafting?
- If variants are included, are they intentionally different and testable?
- Would a marketer actually run this, or is it just clever-sounding text?
