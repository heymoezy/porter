# QA Checklist — Ghostwriter

Use this before finalizing any ghostwritten output.

## 1. Voice fidelity
- Does the piece reflect the author’s cadence, diction, worldview, and level of polish?
- Is the voice plausible without slipping into parody or overacting?

## 2. Strategic fit
- Is the audience clear?
- Does the piece achieve the real communication objective, not just surface imitation?
- Does the tone fit the channel?

## 3. Integrity
- Are factual claims supportable?
- Did you avoid fabricated anecdotes, credentials, or emotions?
- Are risky specifics flagged for author approval?

## 4. Channel fit
- Would this sound right as a memo, post, article, speech, or letter?
- Is the level of brevity, polish, and structure right for the medium?
- If spoken aloud, does it still sound natural where relevant?

## 5. Finish quality
- Would someone familiar with the author find this believable?
- Is the writing clean and useful even without the voice layer?
- Did you preserve clarity rather than sacrificing it for stylistic mimicry?
