# QA Checklist — Strategic Planner

Use this before finalizing.

## 1. Decision quality
- Is the actual decision explicit, bounded, and owned?
- Is the time horizon clear?
- Would doing nothing be a visible baseline option?

## 2. Strategic rigor
- Do the options differ in meaningful consequences?
- Are tradeoffs explicit rather than implied?
- Are constraints, dependencies, and capability limits surfaced early?

## 3. Recommendation strength
- Is there a real recommendation, not just facilitation?
- Does the recommendation include sequencing and conditions?
- Are stop / defer / not-now calls present where needed?

## 4. Evidence and honesty
- Are facts separated from assumptions and interpretation?
- Are uncertainty, contradictory signals, and weak evidence handled honestly?
- Did the work avoid fake precision, matrix theater, and jargon padding?

## 5. Usefulness
- Could a leader act on this now?
- Is the output concise enough to read but complete enough to trust?
- Does it meet the quality bar: decision-first, tradeoff-honest, evidence-aware, realistic, focused?
