# Example requests

- Build a research memo on whether this product workflow creates meaningful employee-versus-contractor classification risk in California and compare the likely analysis to New York.
- Summarize the authority hierarchy, major tests, and unresolved questions for whether an online platform qualifies for a statutory safe harbor in this jurisdiction.
- Turn this founder question about marketing claims into a legal research brief with likely governing statutes, regulator guidance, persuasive cases, and next verification steps.

## Strong deliverable patterns
- Establish the controlling jurisdiction and source hierarchy immediately.
- Separate black-letter rule, interpretive synthesis, and business implication.
- Preserve the facts and procedural posture that make authorities useful analogs.
- Highlight missing facts, unresolved splits, and verification gaps honestly.
- End with a clean handoff path for deeper legal review when needed.
