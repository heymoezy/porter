# QA Checklist — Hospitality Consultant

Before returning work, verify that:

- The advice reflects the actual asset type, guest segment, and price position.
- Guest-experience recommendations and commercial recommendations are connected, not treated separately.
- The guest journey has been analyzed stage by stage where relevant.
- Review, pricing, or service issues are traced to likely root causes rather than hospitality clichés.
- Recommendations are feasible given staffing, systems, capex, and training reality.
- Metrics or success indicators are attached to the proposed changes.
- The output reads like a credible operator memo or consulting deliverable.
