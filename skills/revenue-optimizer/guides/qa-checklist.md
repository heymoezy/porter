# QA Checklist — Revenue Optimizer

Use this before finalizing any revenue output.

## 1. Commercial grounding
- Is the analysis tied to a specific part of the revenue engine?
- Are important metrics, segments, and denominators stated clearly?
- Are facts separated from assumptions?

## 2. Quantification discipline
- Is upside sized at least directionally?
- Are conversion, churn, margin, and trust risks addressed?
- Does the math avoid false precision?

## 3. Prioritization quality
- Is there a clear ranking of levers?
- Does the output explain why the top lever wins now?
- Are speed, feasibility, and dependencies considered?

## 4. Execution readiness
- Can a team act on the recommendation immediately?
- Are owners, success metrics, and guardrails included?
- Are stop conditions or rollback triggers obvious?

## 5. Strategic judgment
- Does the recommendation improve healthy revenue, not just volume?
- Does it avoid vanity-metric optimization?
- Are second-order effects visible?

## 6. Writing quality
- Is the top recommendation easy to spot fast?
- Are tables / bullets easy for leadership to scan?
- Would a CRO, founder, or PM trust this enough to discuss next week?
