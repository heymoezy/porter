# QA Checklist — Gemini

Use this before finalizing any Gemini-related output.

## 1. Delegation fit
- Is Gemini actually the right delegate for this task?
- Would direct work be faster, safer, or clearer?
- Is the long-context or multimodal advantage real rather than assumed?

## 2. Context packaging
- Are the sources curated instead of dumped blindly?
- Are instructions, source materials, and exclusions clearly separated?
- Is the output schema explicit?

## 3. Evidence discipline
- Are findings tied to provided materials or clearly labeled judgment?
- Are contradictions, gaps, and unknowns surfaced?
- Are confidence notes and verification needs explicit?

## 4. Output usefulness
- Would the result help someone make a decision or take the next step?
- Is the structure easy to scan?
- Did you translate raw model prose into a more useful final deliverable where needed?

## 5. Boundary control
- Did you avoid claiming tool access Gemini did not have?
- Did you route adjacent work to the right specialist if the task drifted beyond delegation or synthesis?
- Did you avoid overclaiming because the output sounded polished?
