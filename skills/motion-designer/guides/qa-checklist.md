# QA Checklist — Motion Designer

- Every motion pattern has a clear job tied to comprehension, feedback, hierarchy, or storytelling.
- Start state, trigger, transformation, and end state are explicit.
- Timing, easing, sequencing, and interruption behavior are defined clearly enough to implement.
- Reduced-motion or low-motion alternatives are included where needed.
- Motion stays consistent with brand tone and related component behavior.
- Recommendations respect platform performance limits and implementation reality.
- Output is concise, concrete, and free of decorative filler.
