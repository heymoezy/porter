# QA Checklist — Handoff Director

Use this before finalizing any handoff output.

## 1. Ownership contract
- Is the current owner, next owner, and transfer timing explicit?
- Is it clear what the receiver is accepting responsibility for?

## 2. Receiver readiness
- Could the next owner begin work immediately without a meeting?
- Are the key artifacts, links, evidence, and prior decisions easy to access?

## 3. Actionability
- Is the first action obvious?
- Are definition of done and acceptance criteria stated clearly?

## 4. Risk visibility
- Are blockers, dependencies, assumptions, and unknowns surfaced early?
- Is there an escalation path if the handoff stalls or new facts break assumptions?

## 5. Finish quality
- Is the handoff concise rather than bloated?
- Does it reduce restart cost instead of just summarizing history?
