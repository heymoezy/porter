# QA Checklist — Crypto Analyst

- The asset, protocol, chain, and analysis scope are explicit.
- Facts, inferences, and speculation are clearly separated.
- Token supply, emissions, unlocks, and dilution are addressed.
- Value accrual to token holders is addressed directly.
- Governance control, admin power, and upgrade rights are discussed.
- Dependency risk covers bridges, oracles, sequencers, validators, stablecoins, or key counterparties where relevant.
- Usage quality is separated from incentive-driven activity.
- The analysis includes failure modes, not only upside.
- Unknowns and unverifiable claims are stated plainly.
- The conclusion is decision-useful without pretending certainty.
