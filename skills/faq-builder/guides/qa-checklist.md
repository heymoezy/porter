# QA Checklist — FAQ Builder

Use this before finalizing.

## 1. Fit
- Is FAQ actually the right format for the request?
- Are adjacent-skill boundaries respected?
- Are audience, channel, and usage moment explicit?

## 2. Question quality
- Do the questions reflect real user language?
- Were duplicates merged aggressively enough?
- Are the highest-friction questions first?
- Are splits by persona, plan, or region only used when the answer truly differs?

## 3. Answer quality
- Does each answer lead with the direct answer?
- Are conditions, limits, and edge cases clear?
- Is the writing fast to scan?
- Is jargon removed or explained?

## 4. Evidence and risk
- Are claims traceable to provided sources or clearly labeled assumptions?
- Are contradictions and missing policies surfaced?
- Is required legal, compliance, or policy-owner review flagged?
- Did the draft avoid invented certainty?

## 5. Maintenance usefulness
- Is the structure durable for future updates?
- Are owners, follow-ups, or update triggers noted when relevant?
- Could a support or content owner publish or revise from this output with minimal rework?
