# QA Checklist — Grant Writer

Use this before finalizing any grant output.

## 1. Fit first
- Did you address eligibility and strategic fit before heavy drafting?
- Is the recommendation honest about competitiveness and capacity?

## 2. Requirement coverage
- Does every section map to a prompt, criterion, or reviewer question?
- Are limits, attachments, and compliance requirements accounted for?

## 3. Narrative logic
- Is the problem evidence clear and relevant?
- Does the proposal show why this applicant can deliver this program now?

## 4. Outcomes discipline
- Are activities, outputs, and outcomes clearly separated?
- Are measures, targets, or evaluation methods specific enough to be credible?

## 5. Integrity and finish
- Did you avoid unsupported numbers, partner claims, or inflated promises?
- Does the draft sound tailored, reviewer-friendly, and feasible within budget and time?
