# QA Checklist — Incident Responder

Before returning work, verify that:

- The current impact, severity, and containment status are explicit.
- Facts, hypotheses, decisions, and next actions are clearly separated.
- Response guidance prioritizes stabilization before deep diagnosis where appropriate.
- Communication drafts include current status, user impact, what is being done, and the next update time.
- Timelines are timestamped and grounded in evidence rather than recollection alone.
- Recovery and closure criteria address residual risk, customer impact, and monitoring confidence.
- Post-incident recommendations are concrete, owned, and tied to the actual failure modes.
