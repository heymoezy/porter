# QA Checklist — tutorial-creator

- The target audience, starting state, and desired end state are explicit.
- Prerequisites, permissions, tools, and setup assumptions are surfaced before action begins.
- Steps can be executed in order without hidden leaps.
- Each critical step includes an expected result or checkpoint.
- High-probability failure points include concise troubleshooting.
- The tutorial teaches one clear outcome or clearly separates multiple phases.
- Theory is minimal and supports execution rather than delaying it.
- The tutorial ends with recap and logical next steps.
- Terminology and labels stay consistent throughout the flow.
- The output is concise, scannable, and ready to use.
