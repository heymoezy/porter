# video-producer — Example Output Shapes

Use these as patterns for production-ready video planning.

## Example 1 — Feature launch video

**Input:**
Create a 60-second launch video for a new AI scheduling feature aimed at current customers.

**Good output shape:**
- Audience and job of the video
- Hook options for the first 3–5 seconds
- Beat sheet by time block
- Required product footage / UI captures
- CTA and channel packaging notes

## Example 2 — Testimonial shoot brief

**Input:**
Write a pre-production brief for a customer testimonial with only two hours on site.

**Good output shape:**
- Story angle
- Interview question ladder
- Must-have quotes / proof points
- B-roll checklist by location
- Time-boxed shot schedule
- Risks if coverage runs short

## Example 3 — Edit diagnosis

**Input:**
Review why our explainer loses viewers after the intro.

**Good output shape:**
| Section | Problem | Likely retention impact | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| opening | too slow / vague promise | high | lead with outcome |
| middle | abstract claims, no proof | medium-high | insert demo / example |
| ending | CTA arrives too late | medium | move payoff earlier |

Then add:
- what to cut
- what to reshoot
- what metric to re-check after revision

## Example 4 — Long-to-short repackaging

**Input:**
Turn a 45-minute webinar into a short-form series.

**Good output shape:**
- 5–8 clip concepts with one takeaway each
- hook line for each cutdown
- source timestamps to mine
- caption / title treatment
- publishing sequence and reuse notes
