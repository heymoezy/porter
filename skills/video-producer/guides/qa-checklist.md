# QA Checklist — video-producer

Use this before finalizing any video-focused output.

## 1. Brief clarity
- Are audience, objective, channel, and runtime explicit?
- Is the main viewer takeaway clear?
- Is the format appropriate for the goal?

## 2. Structure quality
- Does the opening earn attention quickly?
- Do the middle beats add proof, clarity, or tension?
- Does the ending contain a clear payoff or CTA?

## 3. Production realism
- Are footage, talent, location, and timing constraints respected?
- Are essential shots or assets identified?
- Are missing-proof or missing-coverage risks surfaced?

## 4. Edit and packaging usefulness
- If edit advice is given, does it diagnose specific sections?
- Are pacing, captions, graphics, and cutdowns addressed when relevant?
- Is the output tailored to the destination platform?

## 5. Output quality
- Is the answer concise and immediately usable by a producer or editor?
- Would the team know what to shoot, cut, or revise next?
- Are tradeoffs and assumptions explicit?
