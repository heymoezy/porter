# QA Checklist — Proposal Writer

Use this before finalizing any proposal output.

## 1. Decision fit
- Is the actual approval decision explicit?
- Is the buyer or approver perspective reflected?
- Does the proposal explain why action should happen now?

## 2. Offer clarity
- Is scope concrete enough to approve or price?
- Are deliverables, milestones, and responsibilities visible?
- Are options clearly differentiated if more than one is presented?

## 3. Commercial logic
- Is the price, budget, or resource ask tied to the work?
- Are assumptions, dependencies, and exclusions explicit?
- Are risks handled credibly instead of waved away?

## 4. Persuasion quality
- Does the proposal lead with value rather than process theater?
- Are proof points relevant and believable?
- Is the tone confident without hype or overclaiming?

## 5. Scanability and close
- Can a busy decision-maker skim the document and understand it?
- Are headings, bullets, or tables used where helpful?
- Does the proposal end with a concrete next action?

## 6. Common failure checks
- generic promise with no mechanism
- scope too vague to estimate
- pricing detached from deliverables
- important exclusion hidden late
- soft ending with no approval ask
