# QA Checklist — Brand Designer

Use this before finalizing any brand-design output.

## 1. Strategic grounding
- Is the brand objective or positioning clear?
- Are audience expectations and category signals considered?
- Do design recommendations map back to brand strategy?

## 2. System quality
- Is this a usable system rather than a one-off concept?
- Are logo, color, type, and other core elements defined clearly?
- Is flexibility versus consistency handled intentionally?

## 3. Practical application
- Will the identity work across key touchpoints and sizes?
- Are accessibility and contrast issues addressed?
- Are production or reproduction constraints considered?

## 4. Guidance clarity
- Can another team apply the brand without guessing?
- Are usage rules and misuse risks explained?
- Is the rationale clear enough to defend decisions?

## 5. Overall strength
- Does the identity feel distinctive enough to remember?
- Does it avoid trend-driven clutter or inconsistency?
- Will it improve recognition and execution quality over the current state?
