# Due Diligence Analyst — Example Deliverable Shapes

Use these patterns to keep outputs decision-ready.

## 1. Investment memo

**Input:**
Should we invest in this vertical SaaS company?

**Strong shape:**
- investment decision and diligence limits
- thesis summary
- claim-versus-evidence table
- revenue quality, retention, and concentration review
- product / market / GTM assessment
- top risks ranked by materiality
- recommendation, confidence, and next checks

## 2. Vendor diligence

**Input:**
Assess this AI vendor before a three-year contract.

**Strong shape:**
- decision criteria tied to the contract
- product maturity and roadmap credibility
- dependency, lock-in, and implementation risk
- security / compliance questions needing specialist follow-up
- commercials and service-risk concerns
- proceed / negotiate / pause recommendation

## 3. Acquisition red-flag pass

**Input:**
Give me the fastest serious diligence pass on this target.

**Strong shape:**
- top 5 immediate red flags
- evidence supporting each flag
- thesis-breaking vs manageable issues
- missing data most likely to change the call
- priority diligence requests for week one
- recommendation with confidence level

## 4. Partnership assessment

**Input:**
Should we pursue this distribution partnership?

**Strong shape:**
- strategic fit and incentive alignment
- channel-quality realism
- exclusivity / concentration / dependency concerns
- operational burden and integration risk
- upside versus coordination cost
- recommended posture and negotiation points

## 5. Founder meeting question pack

**Input:**
Prepare diligence questions for management.

**Strong shape:**
- questions grouped by topic
- what each question is testing
- follow-up probes for evasive answers
- metric definitions to verify
- questions most likely to change valuation or appetite
