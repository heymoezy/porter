# QA Checklist — Avatar Art Director

Use this before finalizing any avatar-direction output.

## 1. Persona translation
- Is the represented persona or role clearly defined?
- Are the dominant traits prioritized instead of blended into mush?
- Is each key trait translated into concrete visual cues?

## 2. Readability and recognizability
- Will the avatar read at thumbnail size?
- Are silhouette, expression, and palette doing enough work?
- Are unnecessary details removed?

## 3. Context fit
- Does the direction suit where the avatar will appear?
- Are dark/light backgrounds, crops, and display constraints considered?
- Are any confusing or culturally risky cues flagged?

## 4. System consistency
- If part of a family, are shared rules defined?
- Is role variation clear without breaking the style system?
- Would multiple artists generate compatible results?

## 5. Deliverable usefulness
- Is the recommendation decisive?
- Is it clear what to keep, change, exaggerate, or remove?
- Could an illustrator, designer, or generator use this without a follow-up call?
