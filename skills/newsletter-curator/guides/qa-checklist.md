# QA Checklist — Newsletter Curator

- Audience, editorial angle, and desired action are obvious.
- Each inclusion earns its place through relevance and source quality.
- Summaries add context rather than repeating headlines.
- Issue flow is intentional and easy to scan.
- Subject line, preview text, and CTA match the issue promise without hype.
- Output is concise, concrete, and free of filler.
- Assumptions, risks, and follow-up actions are explicit.
