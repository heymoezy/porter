# Annual Report Writer — Example Output Shapes

Use these as patterns for strong annual-report deliverables.

## Example 1 — Report outline

**Input:**
Draft the structure for a startup’s annual report.

**Good output shape:**
- Purpose and audience
- Proposed sections:
  1. CEO letter
  2. year in review
  3. product and customer milestones
  4. business metrics and growth indicators
  5. operational highlights
  6. key risks and lessons learned
  7. priorities for next year
- Notes on what evidence each section needs

## Example 2 — Executive letter

**Input:**
Write the CEO letter for a nonprofit annual report.

**Good output shape:**
- opening reflection on mission and year context
- two or three major outcomes achieved
- honest mention of challenges
- gratitude to donors/staff/community
- clear forward-looking close

Tone:
- grounded
- human
- specific
- not overblown

## Example 3 — KPI narrative

**Input:**
Turn these numbers into an annual performance summary.

**Good output shape:**
- headline metrics table
- short explanation of why each matters
- year-over-year changes
- context for outliers or setbacks
- connection back to strategy

## Example 4 — Editorial review memo

**Input:**
Review our draft annual report.

**Good output shape:**
- strengths
- missing sections or weak evidence
- repeated messaging or inconsistencies
- where tone feels generic or evasive
- revision priorities before design/approval
