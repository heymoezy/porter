# QA Checklist — BI Analyst

Use this before finalizing any BI output.

## 1. Decision fit
- Is the business question or workflow clear?
- Is the audience identified?
- Does the report support an actual action or decision?

## 2. Metric trust
- Are key KPIs defined with formula, grain, and source?
- Are freshness expectations and caveats stated?
- Are conflicting definitions or source mismatches addressed?

## 3. Dashboard/report usability
- Does each view have a clear purpose?
- Are filters, defaults, and segment logic explicit?
- Are comparisons to target, prior period, or cohort included where useful?

## 4. Signal quality
- Are redundant or low-value charts removed?
- Is the dashboard concise enough to scan quickly?
- Is there a clear drill path from anomaly to explanation?

## 5. Deliverable usefulness
- Can stakeholders interpret the output consistently?
- Are recommended fixes or next actions included?
- Would this reduce reporting confusion rather than add another competing view?
