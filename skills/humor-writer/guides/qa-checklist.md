# QA Checklist — Humor Writer

Before returning work, verify that:

- The humor matches the audience, channel, and stated risk tolerance.
- Options are meaningfully different in premise or comedic mechanism.
- Each joke has a recognizable setup/payoff, tension/release, or other clear comic engine.
- The wording is concise and performable aloud where relevant.
- The core message survives the comedy pass when clarity matters.
- Potentially offensive, confusing, or off-brand material has been removed or clearly labeled.
- The final set contains at least one standout option rather than a flat list of middling lines.
