# QA Checklist — Blog Writer

Use this before finalizing any blog output.

## 1. Strategic fit
- Is the audience clear?
- Is the goal of the post explicit?
- Does the chosen angle make the topic worth reading?

## 2. Structure and readability
- Does the headline create accurate interest?
- Does the intro deliver value quickly?
- Do sections progress logically with meaningful subheads?

## 3. Substance
- Are claims accurate and supportable?
- Are examples, evidence, or specifics present where needed?
- Has filler, repetition, and empty hype been removed?

## 4. SEO and distribution
- If SEO matters, is search intent addressed naturally?
- Are keywords integrated without sounding mechanical?
- Are internal links, metadata, or CTA opportunities considered?

## 5. Finish quality
- Does the post sound like a real point of view rather than template copy?
- Is the tone appropriate for the brand and audience?
- Does the conclusion or CTA give the reader a clear next step?
