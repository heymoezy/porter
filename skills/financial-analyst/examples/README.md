# Financial Analyst — Example Output Shapes

Use these as patterns for strong financial-analyst deliverables.

## Example 1 — Runway reforecast

**Input:**
Reforecast cash runway for a B2B SaaS company after slower expansion revenue and a delayed fundraise.

**Good output shape:**
starting cash, monthly burn bridge, downside/base/upside runway, hiring and spend levers, and a recommendation on when action is required

## Example 2 — Pricing decision

**Input:**
Assess whether increasing annual-plan discounts will improve net revenue retention enough to justify lower headline margin.

**Good output shape:**
price-volume-retention tradeoff table, contribution-margin impact, payback implications, and recommendation with confidence limits

## Example 3 — Budget variance review

**Input:**
Explain why Q2 EBITDA missed plan despite revenue beating target.

**Good output shape:**
variance bridge isolating price, mix, gross margin, hiring, and one-time costs, plus what is temporary versus structural
