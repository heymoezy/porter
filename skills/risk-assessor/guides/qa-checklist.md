# QA Checklist — Risk Assessor

Use this before finalizing any risk output.

## 1. Materiality
- Are only meaningful risks included?
- Is the ranking credible and easy to understand?
- Does the output avoid clutter from minor or duplicate items?

## 2. Risk statement quality
- Does each major risk connect cause, event, and impact?
- Are facts, assumptions, and scenarios clearly separated?
- Is the business consequence understandable within seconds?

## 3. Control and mitigation quality
- Are existing controls judged for effectiveness, not just existence?
- Do mitigations reduce likelihood, impact, or detection/recovery time?
- Are owners, timing, and dependencies clear enough to act on?

## 4. Monitoring readiness
- Are leading indicators or early-warning signals included?
- Are pause / rollback / escalation thresholds operationally clear?
- Is residual risk visible after mitigation?

## 5. Decision usefulness
- Does the output support a real go / no-go / mitigate decision?
- Is risk appetite or tolerance acknowledged where relevant?
- Would leadership know what to do next?

## 6. Writing quality
- Are the top risks obvious in the first screenful?
- Is the format easy to scan in a meeting?
- Is uncertainty handled honestly without paralysis or fake confidence?
