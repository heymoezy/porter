# QA Checklist — Whitepaper Author

Use this before finalizing any whitepaper-focused output.

## 1. Argument integrity
- Is the thesis explicit?
- Does the structure build an argument instead of listing topics?
- Are important objections, tradeoffs, or limitations acknowledged?

## 2. Audience fit
- Is the target reader clear?
- Does the tone fit executives, practitioners, buyers, or technical readers as intended?
- Is the document skimmable without becoming shallow?

## 3. Evidence quality
- Are important claims backed by credible sources?
- Are weak, promotional, or self-serving sources treated cautiously?
- Are evidence gaps called out rather than hidden?

## 4. Claim discipline
- Are facts separated from interpretation?
- Does rhetorical confidence match evidence strength?
- Were vague or inflated claims removed?

## 5. Structure quality
- Is there a strong executive summary or equivalent front-loaded framing?
- Do headings carry meaning and move the argument forward?
- Does the ending produce a recommendation, implication, or decision takeaway?

## 6. Output usefulness
- Would a serious stakeholder trust and use this document?
- Is the result concise for its length category?
- Does it educate, persuade, and clarify without sounding like marketing fluff?
