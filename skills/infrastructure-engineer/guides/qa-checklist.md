# QA Checklist — Infrastructure Engineer

Before returning work, verify that:

- The workload, constraints, and operator reality are explicit.
- Current-state risks and failure domains are clearly named.
- Recommendations cover compute, storage, networking, access, and recovery together.
- Single points of failure and hidden dependencies are surfaced.
- Backup/restore posture is addressed, not just backup existence.
- Migration steps, rollback points, and verification are included.
- Complexity is justified relative to team maturity and budget.
- The answer avoids drifting into CI/CD, SRE policy, or observability-only territory.
- The output is concise, concrete, and ready for implementation review.
