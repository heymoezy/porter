# Example requests

- Prepare a diligence memo on a vertical SaaS company expanding internationally with slowing net revenue retention.
- Compare two logistics businesses on unit economics, operating leverage, working-capital intensity, and moat durability.
- Build a bull/base/bear view for a marketplace with flattening GMV but improving take rate.
- Review whether a hardware-enabled business deserves a software multiple or an industrial one.

## Strong deliverable patterns
- Start with the actual investment decision and time horizon.
- Separate facts, assumptions, and narrative claims.
- Use scenario tables, sensitivity drivers, and thesis-breaker lists instead of vague prose.
- Make downside concrete, not just upside aspirational.
- End with what to monitor next and what would change the recommendation.
