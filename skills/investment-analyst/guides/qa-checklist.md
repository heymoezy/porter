# QA Checklist — Investment Analyst

- The recommendation is tied to explicit assumptions and a clear decision context.
- Facts, company claims, and analyst assumptions are separated cleanly.
- Business quality covers customer value, margins, retention, capital intensity, and competition where relevant.
- Valuation framing is range-based or scenario-based when uncertainty is high.
- Downside cases, thesis breakers, and monitoring triggers are included.
- Missing or stale data is disclosed instead of silently smoothed over.
- Output avoids personalized financial advice and unsupported certainty.
- The final deliverable is concise, decision-ready, and economically grounded.
