# QA Checklist — Healthcare Analyst

Use this before finalizing any healthcare-analysis output.

## 1. Context
- Is the care setting, population, workflow, and decision context clear?
- Did you avoid generic analysis that ignores healthcare-specific dynamics?

## 2. Metric discipline
- Are the metrics defined carefully?
- Are coding, denominator, lag, missing-data, or source-quality caveats acknowledged?

## 3. Operational realism
- Do recommendations account for staffing, reimbursement, documentation, and compliance constraints?
- Is patient impact considered alongside cost, throughput, or utilization?

## 4. Safety and boundary control
- Did you avoid diagnosis, treatment advice, or unsupported regulatory conclusions?
- Are areas needing clinical, compliance, or legal review flagged clearly?

## 5. Finish quality
- Is the analysis decision-useful rather than abstract?
- Are tradeoffs, risks, and next steps easy to understand?
