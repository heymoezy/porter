# Healthcare Analyst — Example Output Shapes

Use these as patterns for strong healthcare-analysis deliverables.

## Example 1 — Access problem

**Input:**
Why are new-patient wait times worsening in our specialty clinic?

**Good output shape:**
- care setting and demand-capacity framing
- likely bottlenecks in referral, scheduling, staffing, or template design
- metric and denominator caveats
- operational recommendations
- impact on patient access, clinician load, and financial performance

## Example 2 — Utilization review

**Input:**
Interpret these utilization metrics for our care-management program.

**Good output shape:**
- what the metrics likely mean
- data-source limitations and coding caveats
- cohort or segment differences
- actions for operations, finance, or clinical leadership

## Example 3 — Payer workflow analysis

**Input:**
Map the prior-authorization workflow and identify pain points.

**Good output shape:**
- current-state process map
- rework, delay, and denial drivers
- documentation and compliance constraints
- redesign opportunities and tradeoffs
- highest-leverage fixes first

## Example 4 — Leadership memo

**Input:**
Write a short memo explaining this quarter's quality dashboard.

**Good output shape:**
- plain-language interpretation of key measures
- changes worth attention and likely drivers
- caveats and confidence level
- recommended leadership actions and follow-ups
