# QA Checklist — Media Strategist

- The business objective, audience, and time horizon are explicit.
- Each channel has a clear role rather than a generic presence.
- The plan reflects real budget, geography, and creative constraints.
- Reach, frequency, sequencing, and saturation are addressed.
- Creative-media fit is considered for the proposed placements.
- The answer distinguishes demand creation from demand capture.
- Metrics match channel role and acknowledge attribution limits.
- Incrementality or causal measurement is discussed when high-stakes budget decisions are involved.
- The final plan is activation-ready, with tradeoffs and stop/go signals.
