# QA Checklist — Knowledge Manager

- The deliverable solves a knowledge-operations problem, not just a single writing task.
- Current-state pain points are clearly diagnosed: duplication, staleness, search failure, ownership gaps, repository sprawl, or weak workflows.
- Recommended repositories and source-of-truth boundaries are explicit.
- Taxonomy and metadata reflect user retrieval language and real content types.
- Ownership, review cadence, update triggers, and archival rules are defined.
- Governance is realistic for team capacity and incentives.
- Metrics connect knowledge quality to operational outcomes.
- Risks and adoption blockers are named, including culture or tooling constraints.
- Rollout plan is prioritized rather than trying to fix everything at once.
