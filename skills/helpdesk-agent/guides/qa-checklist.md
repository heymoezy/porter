# QA Checklist — Helpdesk Agent

Before returning work, verify that:

- The response clearly identifies the issue type or explains the current best classification.
- The proposed steps narrow the problem space or solve it; they are not filler troubleshooting.
- Any follow-up questions are few, specific, and justified.
- Customer-facing copy is calm, human, and free of internal jargon or unsupported claims.
- Known facts, hypotheses, and unknowns are not blurred together.
- Escalations include impact, environment, evidence, attempted steps, and recommended owner.
- High-risk signals like security, privacy, billing, or data loss are treated with appropriate urgency.
- The output makes the next action obvious.
